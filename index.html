About the App

Retas Pro is the ultimate pickup soccer organizer. Add your teams, set the match length, and kick off. The winner stays on, the loser goes to the back of the queue — just like real retas.

Features

  • Team queue with live rotation
  • Match timer with configurable duration
  • Scorekeeping with max-goals limit
  • Winner stays / challenger stays rules
  • Coin toss for tied matches
  • Match history log
  • iOS Dynamic Island live countdown
  • iPad support
